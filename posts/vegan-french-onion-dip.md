## Ingredients

- 12 oz container vegan sour cream (e.g. Tofutti brand)
- 1&frac12; Tbsp dried chopped onions
- 1 tsp miso (yellow or white)
- 1 tsp soy sauce
- &frac14; tsp salt
- Pinch garlic powder

## Directions

Mix everything together.
